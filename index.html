About Mourish Education

Mourish Education is a specialized bot school dedicated to teaching students from Grade 1 to Grade 12 block-based coding, app development, and web development in online classes. Our goal is to build strong programming foundations and foster creativity in technology through hands-on learning and dynamic projects.

Our Courses

  • Block-Based Coding: Interactive visual programming to learn logical thinking and problem-solving.
  • App Development: Building mobile apps using user-friendly tools and coding practices.
  • Web Development: Designing and coding websites with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript fundamentals.

Courses are tailored progressively to match the skill levels of students in each grade.
